Turbulence Assurance

Stay informed about in‑flight turbulence and how to manage it calmly.

Current turbulence:

Mild – You may feel slight bumps.

Expected turbulence:

Moderate – Keep seatbelts fastened.

Dos & Don’ts

  • Do remain seated with your seatbelt fastened.
  • Do listen to instructions from the crew.
  • Don’t stand or walk unless necessary.
  • Don’t panic – turbulence is normal and aircraft are designed to handle it.

Play some calming music and focus on your breath to stay centred.
